PyTorch Governance | Maintainers

Responsibilities

- Triage and fix high priority issues assigned to the module or library
- Triage, review, and land high priority pull requests assigned to the
  module or library
- Answer module or library questions on discuss.pytorch.org and
  dev-discuss.pytorch.org
- Maintain public user and development documentation
- Run meetings and share minutes plus roadmap on a half or quarterly
  basis
